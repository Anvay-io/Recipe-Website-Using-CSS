## 🥗 ABOUT FLAVORA
**Flavora** is a beautifully crafted recipe repository designed for food lovers, home cooks, and culinary enthusiasts. Our mission is to make cooking accessible, enjoyable, and creative—whether you are mastering a classic Dosa or preparing a vibrant Paneer dish.

## 🥘 THE MENU (Project Highlights)
*   **Curated Classics:** A selection of authentic recipes with clear, step-by-step instructions.
*   **User-Centric Design:** Clean, distraction-free layout to help you focus on the cooking process.
*   **Interactive Experience:** Featuring intuitive overlays and a responsive design that works perfectly on desktop and mobile.
*   **Global Inspiration:** From traditional Indian staples to everyday meals, we celebrate the art of cooking.

## 🛠️ THE PANTRY (Tech Stack)
*   **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Custom responsive styling)
*   **Logic:** Structured DOM with semantic HTML for better readability
*   **Assets:** Optimized images for a fast-loading culinary experience

## 🍳 HOW TO EXPLORE
1. **Browse:** Visit the main page to see our latest featured recipes.
2. **Select:** Click on any dish card to view detailed ingredients and step-by-step instructions.
3. **Connect:** Visit the "About" section to learn our story or use the "Contact" section to share your own recipes and feedback.
